Personal Summary

Expert software engineer with over 20 years experience delivering complex IT solutions across diverse industries.

Specialist Business Analyst, Requirements Engineer and Product Owner with over 10 years experience in the precise definition, elicitation, documentation and management of requirements. I am CPRE certified and coauthor/examiner for IREB RE@Agile Advanced Level.

That is, I can offer real insights to help your business uncover hidden needs and opportunities to be addressed by innovative IT solutions – and proven skills in managing the complexity of delivering those solutions.

Vision and Purpose

As a senior professional I am motivated both by the opportunity of applying tried and tested skills to new challenges in business and technology, and by the possibility of sharing my insights and experience to motivate and educate others (see below).

I believe in professionalising the discipline of requirements engineering by providing a certified path to support measurable career progression (see below).

And I believe in using technology to create innovative, well-designed and sustainable solutions that help businesses to operate efficiently and that have a positive impact on the people that use them and the societies in which they live.
